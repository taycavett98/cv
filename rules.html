time management rules

  1. set a bed time
  2. plan your week on friday
  3. move by 3pm
  4. 3x a week as a habit : see your life in weeks, not days
  5. 1 big adventure and 1 little adventure each week - out of the ordinary things to create memories
  6. effortful before effortless - make time to read first, then doomscroll
  7. block out time for little tasks (scheduling appointments/ litte chores)
